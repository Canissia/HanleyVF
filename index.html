About Us

Graduating from Full Sail University with my Bachelor of Science Degree in Digital Film was one of my greatest accomplishments. I have gained over 4 years of professional videography experience by filming a variety of events such as sports games, commercials, short films, promotional videos and weddings.

I fell in love with love! And now, creating dynamic wedding videos has become my favorite thing to do. I like to make my videos personalized to each couple. For an example, all of my couples have personally selected the songs they wanted for their highlight video. Also, I am flexible with the lengths of feature videos and highlights to fit my couples preference.
